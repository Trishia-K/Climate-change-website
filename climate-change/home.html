Overview of Climatic Regions in Uganda

Uganda's climate is surprisingly diverse for its size with;
Tropical with a twist:
Most of Uganda is tropical with high temperatures year round and a wet-dry season pattern. The twist is the number of rainy seasons.
North Vs South:
The north is semi arid with one rainy season while the south is humid with two rainy seasons. Rainfall increases as you go South.
Mountains matter:
Uganda has mountains that are colder than the lowlands.
Lake effect:
The large lakes like Victoria contribute to overall humidity and local weather patterns.
